# Lm sch d liu
df = df.dropna()
df = df[df['Close'] != 0]

# Tnh cc ng trung bnh ng
df['MA10'] = df['Close'].rolling(window=10).mean()
df['MA20'] = df['Close'].rolling(window=20).mean()
df['MA50'] = df['Close'].rolling(window=50).mean()
df['MA100'] = df['Close'].rolling(window=100).mean()
df['MA200'] = df['Close'].rolling(window=200).mean()

# V biu  gi ng ca v cc ng MA
plt.figure(figsize=(12, 8))
plt.plot(df['Close'], label='Close', color='blue')
plt.plot(df['MA10'], label='MA10')
plt.plot(df['MA20'], label='MA20')
plt.plot(df['MA50'], label='MA50')
plt.plot(df['MA100'], label='MA100')
plt.plot(df['MA200'], label='MA200')

# Thit lp nhn v tiu 
plt.title('Biu  gi c phiu AAPL v cc ng MA')
plt.xlabel('Ngy')
plt.ylabel('Gi ng ca (USD)')
plt.legend(loc='upper left', fontsize=12)
plt.gcf().autofmt_xdate()
plt.tight_layout()
plt.savefig('AAPL_plot.png', dpi=300)
plt.show()

Mc ch:
on code ny thc hin lm sch d liu, tnh cc ng trung bnh ng (Moving Averages MA), v v biu gi c phiu AAPL quan st xu hng bin ng gi theo thi gian.

C th:

  • df.dropna() v df[df['Close'] != 0]: loi b cc gi tr b thiu hoc nhng ngy c gi ng ca bng 0, m bo d liu sch v hp l.
  • df['MA10'], df['MA20'], df['MA50'], df['MA100'], df['MA200']: to cc ct trung bnh ng trong 10, 20, 50, 100 v 200 ngy gip nhn bit xu hng ngn, trung v di hn.
  • Biu :
    • ng mu xanh dng th hin gi ng ca thc t.
    • Cc ng MA gip lm mt chui gi, h tr nh u t xc nh xu hng tng/gim ca c phiu.
  • Cui cng, plt.savefig('AAPL_plot.png') lu biu thnh file nh s dng hoc bo co.

ngha:
Vic kt hp cc ng trung bnh ng gip nh gi xu hng th trng v d, khi ng MA ngn hn ct ln MA di hn, c th bo hiu tn hiu mua; ngc li, l tn hiu bn.
